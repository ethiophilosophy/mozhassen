About Me

I am Mohammed Zeinu Hassen, currently a lecturer of Philosophy at Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, Department of Social Sciences. I hold a Master of Arts in Philosophy and a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy (major) with a minor in Political Science and International Relations, both from Addis Ababa University.

My continuous interests include Ethiopian Philosophy, Continental Philosophy, Philosophical Counseling, Comparative Philosophy & Religion, Multiculturalism, Philosophy and Films, and Artificial Intelligence and Law. I am also a blogger at dssaastu.github.io.
